# CukaiPro – Complete user guide

This guide is for new users of CukaiPro: business owners, accountants, and staff who need to manage Malaysian tax, invoices, deductions, and filings in one place. You will learn how to sign up, complete onboarding, use the dashboard, manage invoices, work with taxes (including SST and deductions), view reports, manage your team, and get help.

**Need support?** Contact [support@cukaipro.my](mailto:support@cukaipro.my).

For a list of screenshots to capture or refresh, see [Screenshot checklist](GUIDE_SCREENSHOTS.md).

## Part 1: Getting started

### Pricing and plans

On the **landing page**, use the **Pricing** link in the navigation (or scroll down) to open the **pricing section**. There you will see:

- **Start using CukaiPro for free!** – A short intro with bullets: *Cancel any time*, *No minimum contract*, *No credit card required*, and a **Start Your Free Trial** button.
- **Choose Your Pricing Plan** – Three billing options: **Monthly**, **12 Months 65% Off**, and **24 Months 75% Off**.
- A **comparison table** for **Free**, **Pro**, and **Enterprise** plans, with:
  - **Best for** (e.g. Freelancers & Startups, Growing Businesses, Large Organizations)
  - **Included users** (1 user, up to 5 users, or unlimited)
  - **Tax & compliance** (Accounting & Reports, SST Filing, LHDN e-Invoice, EA Forms)
  - **Support** and **Attachment** (storage)
  - A CTA in each column: **Get Started Free**, **Start Free Trial**, or **Contact Sales**

A footer note states that prices exclude SST and that all promotion offers are subject to terms and conditions.

![Landing – Pricing section](public/guide/guide-landing-pricing.png)

### Create your account

1. Open the CukaiPro app (e.g. your deployed URL or `http://localhost:3000`).
2. On the **landing page**, click **Sign up** (or go to the sign-up page). You can also go to **Pricing** (in the nav or by scrolling) to compare plans and click **Start Your Free Trial** or **Get Started Free**.

   ![Landing page](public/guide/guide-landing.png)

3. On the **Create your account** form, enter:
   - **Full name** – Your name (at least 2 characters).
   - **Company name** – Your business name (e.g. “My Sdn Bhd”).
   - **Email address** – A valid email you will use to sign in.
   - **Password** – At least 8 characters, with uppercase, lowercase, and a number.
4. Click **Create account**.

   ![Sign up form](public/guide/guide-signup.png)

5. After sign-up you are taken to **onboarding**. If you already have an account, use **Sign in** instead.

### Log in

1. Open the app and go to the **Login** page.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Click **Sign in**.

   ![Login page](public/guide/guide-login.png)

4. If you have already completed onboarding, you go straight to the **Dashboard**. Otherwise you will see the onboarding steps.

### Onboarding (first-time setup)

After sign-up you go through four short steps. You can skip optional steps and complete them later from the dashboard.

- **Step 1 – Welcome**  
  Click **Get started** to begin.

  ![Onboarding – Welcome](public/guide/guide-onboarding-welcome.png)

- **Step 2 – Create your organization**  
  Enter your **business name** (or confirm the one shown if you already have an organization), then click **Continue**.

  ![Onboarding – Organization](public/guide/guide-onboarding-org.png)

- **Step 3 – Add your first deduction (optional)**  
  You can add one tax-deductible expense now: choose a **category**, enter **amount (RM)** and optionally a description, then click **Add deduction**. Or click **Skip for now** to do this later from **Taxes → Deductions**.

  ![Onboarding – First deduction](public/guide/guide-onboarding-deduction.png)

- **Step 4 – Invite your team (optional)**  
  Enter a teammate’s **email** and **role** (Staff, Admin, or Accountant), then click **Send invite**. Or click **Skip for now** or **Continue to dashboard** to finish. You can invite more people later from **Settings → Team**.

  ![Onboarding – Invite team](public/guide/guide-onboarding-invite.png)

When you finish step 4 (or skip it), you are taken to the **Dashboard**.

## Part 2: Dashboard

The dashboard is your home after login. It gives a quick view of:

- **SST (Sales and Service Tax)** – Next due date and amount (if applicable).
- **Revenue** – Overview of income and recent activity.
- **Recent expenses** – Latest invoices/expenses.
- **Pay Now** – Quick access to pay SST or other dues.
- **Download Report** – Export a report for the current period.
- **Support** – Open the in-app support chat if you need help.

![Dashboard](public/guide/guide-dashboard.png)

### Navigation (sidebar)

Use the **sidebar** on the left to move between sections:

- **Dashboard** – This overview.
- **Taxes** – Expandable menu:
  - **Overview** – Tax year and summary.
  - **SST Filing** – SST returns and due dates.
  - **Deductions** – Add and manage tax deductions.
  - **Filing Summary** – Annual filing / Form B preparation.
- **Invoices** – Create and manage invoices.
- **Reports** – Tax and filing reports.
- **Help** – FAQs and support.
- **Settings** – Profile and team.

At the bottom of the sidebar you see your **current organization** and **Sign out**.

![Sidebar navigation](public/guide/guide-sidebar.png)

## Part 3: Invoices

Invoices help you track sales and income for reporting and SST.

1. Click **Invoices** in the sidebar.
2. Create, view, and manage invoices: add line items, tax rates, and customer details.
3. Use the list to filter, search, and export or share as needed.

For more detail, see the FAQ: **How do I manage invoices?** on the Help page.

## Part 4: Taxes

### Overview

- Go to **Taxes → Overview**.
- Use the **year selector** to switch tax year (Malaysian tax year is Jan–Dec).
- Review your tax position and summary for the selected year.

![Taxes overview](public/guide/guide-taxes-overview.png)

### SST Filing

If your business is SST-registered:

- Go to **Taxes → SST Filing**.
- See **due dates** and **filing periods** (typically every 2 months).
- Use the dashboard and this page to prepare and track SST returns for RMCD.

![SST Filing](public/guide/guide-sst-filing.png)

### Deductions

- Go to **Taxes → Deductions**.
- Click **Add Deduction**.
- Choose a **category** (e.g. business expenses, capital allowance, personal relief), enter **amount (RM)** and **date** (DD/MM/YYYY), and optionally upload a **receipt**.
- Save; the claimable amount is calculated according to LHDN rules.

![Deductions](public/guide/guide-deductions.png)

### Filing Summary

- Go to **Taxes → Filing Summary**.
- Use this view to prepare your **annual tax filing** and **Form B** data (business income, allowable expenses, tax payable). Filing is due by 30 June (or as extended).

![Filing summary](public/guide/guide-filing-summary.png)

## Part 5: Reports

- Go to **Reports** in the sidebar.
- View an overview of **tax filings**, **deductions**, **SST**, and **EA forms**.
- Use this together with **Filing Summary** to track progress and prepare for LHDN submissions.

![Reports](public/guide/guide-reports.png)

---

## Part 6: Settings and team

- Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
- **Profile** – Update your account details if available.
- **Team** – Invite and manage team members:
  - Enter the invitee’s **email** and select a **role** (Staff, Admin, Accountant).
  - Click **Send invite**. The invitee receives a link; they must accept to join.
  - Only Admins and Owners can send invites.

![Settings – Team](public/guide/guide-settings-team.png)

---

## Part 7: Help and support

- Go to **Help** in the sidebar.
- **Search** the FAQs (e.g. “deductions”, “SST”, “EA form”).
- **Expand** any question to read the answer.
- Use **Contact support** to email [support@cukaipro.my](mailto:support@cukaipro.my) for CukaiPro or Malaysian tax questions.

![Help & FAQ](public/guide/guide-help.png)
